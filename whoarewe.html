"Music washes away from the soul the dust of everyday life." - Berthold Auerbach

Our Mission

Epiphany was created to connect residents in level of care homes with UT student volunteers though personalized art performances, improving their quality of life. We want to help people in need through the new fields of musical and artistic therapy, as well as provide students and other artists an avenue with which they can use their talents to better the community. We intend to make the process of communication between residents and artists much more streamlined through the creation of a mobile application, while also raising awareness for the problems that memory patients face. Within a few years, we aim to foster a community of caring and giving around this issue, with the intent of improving society as a whole.
