What We Do

Epiphany was made to connect level of care residents to UT volunteers
in music and other fine arts to improve their quality of life. Through our
web and mobile applications, we aim to provide reliable information to
volunteers and recipients alike about people around them to provide or
receive musical and artistic therapy.
